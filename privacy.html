Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Type It ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our construction document processing service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email address)
  • Construction documents and drawings you upload
  • Payment information (processed securely by our payment provider)
  • Usage data and analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your construction documents and generate drawing schedules
  • Provide customer support
  • Send service updates and notifications
  • Improve our services
  • Detect and prevent fraud

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for all uploaded documents
  • Secure data storage on encrypted servers
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to user data

5. AI Training

We do NOT use your construction documents to train AI models. Your project documents remain private and are only processed to generate your requested drawing schedules.

6. Data Retention

We automatically delete your uploaded documents based on your plan:

  • Starter plan: 30 days after processing
  • Pay As You Go: 90 days after processing
  • Professional plan: Unlimited storage (or until you delete)

You can delete your files at any time from your account dashboard.

7. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for:

  • Payment processing (Stripe)
  • Cloud storage and computing
  • Email delivery
  • Analytics

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
